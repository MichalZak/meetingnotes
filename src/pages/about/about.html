Meeting Notes

It has been stated many times taking brief notes will help us pay attentions and remember vital spiritual truths. On top of that, having notes saved, will allow us to review a talk that touched us, or help us find an illustration that we heard before at a meeting, but can't remember its details.

The goal of this application is to help us make better notes at theocratic meetings. As we make more and more notes, allow us to search through these notes to find certain verse explanations or illustrations that we have heard in past meetings.

Implemented:

  • Note: Create notes
  • About: Application introduction, and feature summary
